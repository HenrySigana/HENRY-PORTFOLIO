Bio

I run GB ICT Solutions — a cyber cafe and M-Pesa agency in Nairobi — and I also build the software that keeps it running. That dual life is the whole practice: every tool I ship gets used at my own counter first, long before a client ever sees it.

What started as managing my own till and shop records turned into a habit of building small, focused tools whenever something was slow or manual. Over time that habit became a portfolio: retail POS, pharmacy stock, construction site tracking, hospitality management, property listings — each one solving a real problem I'd either hit myself or watched a neighbouring business struggle with.

My stack is deliberately plain — vanilla JavaScript, Supabase for real-time sync, GitHub Pages and Netlify for deployment. No unnecessary frameworks, just fast tools that survive patchy Nairobi internet and still sync the moment it's back.
